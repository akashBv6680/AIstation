FAQ

Everything you wanted to ask

How exactly do credits work?

You buy credits once. ₹15 = 10 minutes on any tool. Switch between tools as often as you like — your credits are universal. They never expire.

Can I bring my own laptop?

Yes — you can plug in via Ethernet or use our Wi-Fi. But our workstations are tuned for AI workflows (high-end GPU, 4K screens, fast SSD), so most people prefer them.

Are accounts shared between users?

Each session runs in an isolated, sandboxed profile. Your prompts and history are wiped at logout. We never store or share your work without permission.

What if the tool I want is busy?

Almost never happens — we license multiple seats per tool. If a popular tool hits capacity, we pause your timer until a slot opens. You don't lose credits.

Do you offer student or team discounts?

Yes. Show a college ID for 20% off any pack. Team passes (5+ users) get 25% off — email us for an invoice and GST.

Can I take my work home?

Absolutely. Save outputs to a USB drive, your Google Drive, Dropbox, or email them to yourself. We just don't keep anything on our side.

What about refunds?

Unused credits are fully refundable within 30 days, no questions asked. Just walk in and ask any staff member.

Is this only for tech people?

Not at all. About half our visitors are first-timers — students, designers, marketers, teachers, even retired folks exploring AI for the first time.
